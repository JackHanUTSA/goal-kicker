## Structured extraction
- Majors count: 37
- Count method: official Princeton Admission Degrees & Departments page states students can choose from among 37 concentrations
- Testing policy: Princeton is test-optional for first-year applicants applying for fall 2026 and fall 2027 entry; Princeton says it will once again require testing for students applying for fall 2028 entry.
- GPA policy: Princeton does not publish a minimum GPA on the cited admissions pages.
- Course rigor: No simple minimum course-rigor formula is stated on the cited Princeton pages, but Princeton asks for teacher recommendations from higher level courses in core academic areas.
- Recommendations: Princeton requires a counselor recommendation plus two teacher recommendations from higher level courses in different academic areas.
- Essays: Princeton requires Princeton-specific questions and a graded written paper for first-year applicants.
