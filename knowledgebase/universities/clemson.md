## Structured extraction
- Majors count: 76
- Count method: counted bachelor-level program titles from Clemson's official Program Finder with the Bachelor's Degree filter enabled
- Testing policy: Clemson has adopted a test-optional policy. Applicants are not required to submit SAT or ACT scores to be considered for admission, but if they want scores used in review, Clemson requires official scores sent electronically by the testing agency.
- GPA policy: Clemson reviews GPA, class rank, and course rigor, but the cited official admissions pages do not state a minimum GPA requirement.
- Course rigor: Clemson says one of the clearest indicators of fit is academic performance in high school and that it examines GPA, class rank, and the rigor of classes taken. It also says most applicants present college-preparatory, honors, and AP coursework based on what their high school offers.
- Recommendations: Clemson does not require letters of recommendation for admission, but applicants may upload letters of recommendation as optional supplemental materials in the Clemson admissions portal after submitting the application.
- Essays: Clemson does not require the SAT or ACT essay, and it says personal statements and other supplemental materials are optional rather than required for admission.
