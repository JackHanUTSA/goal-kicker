## Structured extraction
- Majors count: 100
- Count method: counted unique Stanford Explore Majors opportunity pages on the official text-only list, excluding the one explicit minor-labeled entry 'Dance (TAPS Minor)'
- Testing policy: ACT or SAT scores are required for first-year applicants. Stanford says there are no minimum test scores required for admission and no score that guarantees admission.
- GPA policy: Stanford does not publish a minimum GPA cutoff on its first-year admissions pages; instead, it says courses and grades matter and points applicants to rigorous academic preparation.
- Course rigor: Stanford recommends rigorous coursework each year, roughly five academic courses per semester, and strong performance across English, math, social studies, science, and world language.
- Recommendations: Stanford requires a school report form and counselor recommendation plus letters of recommendation from two academic teachers.
- Essays: Applicants submit the Common Application personal essay and Stanford Questions, including several short questions plus three short essays of 100 to 250 words each.
